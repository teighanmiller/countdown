How to Play

3 × Letter Rounds
Nine letters are revealed. Find the longest valid word you can in 30 seconds, then type it in 10 seconds.

3 × Number Rounds
Hit a target number using 6 available numbers with +, −, ×, ÷. Closer to the target = more points.

Theme Guess
What secret theme connects all the rounds? Guess it after round 6 — the closer, the more points.

Conundrum
A scrambled 9-letter word. First player to unscramble it wins 10 bonus points.
